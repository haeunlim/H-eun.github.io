Rare minerals and semi-minerals with high aesthetic value are those
that have been processed and used as jewelry. Usually, they are
non-metallic minerals with a certain chemical composition, but there
are no strict academic standards and the key is how much people
prefer them in society. Organic matter such as pearls, corals, and
pumpkins can also be used as jewelry, and it can be said that
certain substances that are currently treated as jewelry are not
used as jewelry if people's preferences change and they are not
treated as jewelry. This is because "aesthetic value" is the core.
